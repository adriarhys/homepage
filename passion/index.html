basic moves

plié "plee-AY" to bend
tondu "tan-do" to stretch
rond de jambe "rawn duh zhahnb" circle of the leg
grand battement "grahn bat-MAHN" large beat
assemblé "a-sahn-BLAY" to assemble
balancé "ba-lahn-SAY" rocking step
pas de chat "pah duh shah" step of the cat
penché "pahn-SHAY" leaning or flexed
pirouette "peer-WET" to whirl or spin
port de bras "pawr duh brah" > carriage of the arms
